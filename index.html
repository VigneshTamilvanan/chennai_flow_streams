🚀 Get Started in 3 Steps
  • 1. Set preferences — tap the blue bar at the bottom, pick your budget, commute & lifestyle needs
  • 2. Explore the map — zone markers glow green → yellow → red by liveability; tap any for a full area report
  • 3. Get Top Picks — hit "Show My Best Zones" for a ranked shortlist tailored to you
🗺️ What's on the Map
  • Drop a custom pin anywhere for an instant flood & area report
  • Toggle flood streams, metro lines, roads & parks/lakes via the Layers menu (top-right)
  • Switch basemap between Hybrid satellite, Google Normal or OSM
  • Sort Top Picks by score, commute time or price
📊 Data Behind the Scores
  • Flood risk — ALOS PALSAR 12.5 m DEM stream network + historical events
  • Property rates — TN Registration Dept. guideline values (₹/sq ft)
  • Commute — Haversine × 1.35 road factor at 20 km/h peak average
  • Facilities — OpenStreetMap Overpass API (live query)
  • Metro — CMRL Phase 1, 2 & approved Phase 3 corridors
